2. What To Include In A Support Request

To help us troubleshoot faster, include as much of the following as you can:

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • The version of Sudoku & Chill you are using.
  • A short description of what happened and the steps to reproduce it.
  • A screenshot or screen recording, if available.

3. Common Topics

  • Saved progress: saved games and stats are stored locally on your device.
  • Gameplay questions: if a hint or puzzle state seems confusing, send a screenshot and a short note.
  • Bug reports: include what you tapped just before the issue appeared.
  • Optional tips: tips are optional in-app purchases processed by Apple through the App Store.
  • Billing or refunds: for payment, billing, or refund issues, contact Apple through reportaproblem.apple.com.
  • Restoring tips: if your tip products are consumable, they generally are not restorable after purchase.
