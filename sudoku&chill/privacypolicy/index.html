Sudoku & Chill

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

At a glance: the current version of Sudoku & Chill stores gameplay progress, settings, and player stats locally on your device. It does not require an account, does not use advertising SDKs, does not use third-party analytics or crash-reporting SDKs, and does not track you across apps or websites.

No account required You can play without signing up or creating a profile.
Local-only gameplay data Saved games, preferences, and stats stay in the app's local storage on your device.
No cross-app tracking The app does not use ATT-based tracking for the current release.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Juan Manuel Gomez Llanos ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use the Sudoku & Chill iOS app (the "App").

The App is designed to work primarily on-device. Based on the current release configuration, gameplay data and settings remain local to your device unless you choose to interact with Apple services or open this website.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Saved games, puzzle progress, and related in-game state.
  • App and gameplay preferences, such as appearance, hints, haptics, and autosave options.
  • Local player statistics, such as completed games, score history, and elapsed time.
  • Optional local support history, such as whether you previously sent a tip through Apple's in-app purchase system.

Current release behavior

This information is stored in the App's local container on your device and is not sent to us by the App as part of normal gameplay.

3. Information We Do Not Collect Through the App

  • We do not require account creation or sign-in.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or mailing address through the App.
  • We do not collect precise location data through the App.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs in the current release.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or crash-reporting SDKs in the current release.
  • We do not track you across third-party apps, websites, or services.

4. Apple Services and External Links

The App can present Apple-managed system features, such as an App Store rating prompt, and it can open external links when you choose to visit this Privacy Policy page.

If you choose to send an optional tip, the purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. We do not collect your payment card details through the App.

Any information processed by Apple or another destination website when you use those features is governed by that party's own terms and privacy practices, not by this App's local gameplay storage.

5. Third-Party Services

The current version of Sudoku & Chill does not integrate third-party advertising, analytics, or crash-reporting SDKs.

If a future release adds a service that receives data from the App, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures will be updated before that release is made available.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Because the App stores gameplay data locally on your device, that data remains there until you delete it.

  • You can remove individual saved games using the controls available in the App.
  • You can remove the App from your device to delete the App's local data, subject to how iOS handles backups and restoration.

7. Children's Privacy

Sudoku & Chill is a general-audience puzzle game. The current version of the App is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children through the App.

If you believe there is a privacy concern involving a child and this App, please contact us using the details below.

8. Your Choices and Privacy Rights

Because the current version of the App does not send gameplay data from your device to our servers, most privacy choices relate to information stored locally on your device.

You can manage or delete that information by changing in-app settings, deleting saved games, or removing the App from your device.

9. Security

We use reasonable measures appropriate to a local-only app design. However, no device, software environment, or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App's features or data practices change. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date on this page.
